Sugeng rawuh! (javanese: Welcome!). I'm an undergraduate researcher. I've been working on a research project as a full-time researcher while studying at the Purple Campus.

Currently, I'm collaborating with Prof. Derry Wijaya of Boston University, Dr. Kumara Ari Yuana, and Dr. (Cand.) Arif Dwi Laksito from Universitas Amikom Yogyakarta. On a project building an exciting platform for collecting and digitizing language resource to preserve Indonesian local languages, named Ragambahasa. This platform reveals many new research ideas, problems, and opportunities!

This project as part of the US-Indonesia Research Collaboration Program. Funded by the Indonesian Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology (MoECRT) and the Indonesia Endowment Fund for Education (LPDP), this program fosters collaboration between researchers from the US and Indonesia.

Previously, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Kusrini, Dr. Arief Setyanto, and several other researchers on a research project named SILVANUS. Funded by the European Horizon program in 2020, this project's main goal is to manage wildfires by leveraging an integrated technological and information platform.

Prior to that, I worked as a Data Engineer for a Big Data Solutions Company in Yogyakarta, Indonesia. My primary task was handling data flows from upstream to downstream.
